TOM WELLBY

Qualified Personal Trainer - Bachelor of Science (honours), Brunel University, UK.
Tom had the opportunity to work in the health and fitness industry since he was 16 years old. Foundations are the same since then, to reach targets it requieres hard work. However, the tools to get those achievements have improve during the years, making it more enjoyable.

HIS CARREAR

Tom has an extensive sporty back ground, either competing or working alongside athletes, from triathlon, duathlon, swimming, running and surfing.

He was a professional cyclist for 8 years, when this stage of his life came to an end he raced kytesurfing when the early stages of this sport started, being a research and development rider around the globe.

Tom’s phylosophy of fitness

When you are fit and healthy it lays a domino effect on the rest of your life. There are many factors involve in making up a healthy body. Over the years I have construct my beliefs and discover diferent options. We are all unique, making everyone of us diferent from each other, so there is not an exclusive formula that will work for all of us. That is the exciting part of Tom’s work, to get into people’s health and help them to find their formula.

“Helping clients to become more confident or to run their first 5Km race, puts a massive smile on
my face and a beautiful memory. That’s why I do what i do” T.W.
