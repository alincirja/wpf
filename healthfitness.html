HEALTH & FITNESS

What is to be healthy?

For some is being able to run a marathon and for others is to go for a daily walk.
The balance of a physycal, mental and spirirutual wellbeing are all part of being a healthy person. Without leaving aside a good diet that ensures to fill ourselves with all the required nutrients and healthy digestion that promote regular bathroom visits. Along with all those factors the environment we live in, including our social life, work and regular routine affect our comfort. Lastly, the most important ingredient for human harmony is happiness, which is generally holding hands with health and viceversa.

I believe that to be healthy, we need to be active and for that we need energy, which is found everywhere around us such as in what we eat and our surroundings. Indeed, mountains, oceans, deserts, forests, etc. are extremely powerful to tune us with ourselves and what is around us.

In any case, we all have the ability to take control of all the aspects of good health.

What is fitness?

There are many components to physical fitness, every person choses which one to focus the most.
I personally prioritize the followings:

  • Cardiorespiratory Endurance
  • Muscle strengh
  • Muscle endurance
  • Flexibility
  • Body Composition

  • The ability of both body systems (cardiocirculatory and repiratory) to deliver oxygen and nutrients to the muscles, and the capability of removing the produced waste from the cells after a physical effort.
